Privacy Policy for HomeTracker

Last updated: 7/17/2026

HomeTracker is built to respect your privacy. 
This policy explains what data we collect and how 
we use it.

DATA STORAGE
All data you enter into HomeTracker — including 
home details, room information, appliances, tasks, 
photos, and notes — is stored locally on your 
device. We do not collect, transmit, or have access 
to any of your personal data.

PHOTOS
HomeTracker requests access to your photo library 
solely to allow you to attach photos to appliances 
and home systems. Photos are stored on your device 
and are never uploaded or shared.

NOTIFICATIONS
HomeTracker requests permission to send local 
notifications to remind you of upcoming maintenance 
tasks. These notifications are generated on your 
device and do not involve any external servers.

THIRD PARTY SERVICES
HomeTracker does not use any third-party analytics, 
advertising, or tracking services.

CHILDREN
HomeTracker is not directed at children under 13 
and does not knowingly collect data from children.

CHANGES
We may update this policy from time to time. 
Any changes will be posted at this URL.
